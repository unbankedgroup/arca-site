## GoHighLevel (GHL)

**Best for:** Marketing agencies, funnel-heavy businesses, tech-savvy users who want full control.

### Pricing

| Plan | Monthly Cost | What You Get |
|------|--------------|--------------|
| Agency Starter | $97 | Unlimited contacts, basic features, per-message charges for email/SMS |
| Agency Unlimited | $297 | Everything + white label, API access |
| Agency Pro | $497 | Advanced features, SaaS mode |

**Hidden costs:** Email and SMS charges can double or triple your effective cost. At scale, expect $200-500/month in messaging fees alone.

### Setup time

**30-40 hours** for a functional setup. This includes:
- Configuring pipelines and triggers
- Building funnels and landing pages
- Setting up email/SMS sequences
- Connecting integrations via Zapier or native

GoHighLevel is a builder tool. You get a blank canvas and 200+ features. The learning curve is real.

### Strengths

- Extremely powerful automation builder
- Full funnel and landing page builder
- White-label agency capabilities
- Massive feature set

### Weaknesses

- Steep learning curve
- Built for agencies, not coaches/consultants
- Per-message pricing adds up quickly
- Requires technical comfort or hiring a specialist

### Best use case

You're a marketing agency managing multiple client accounts. Or you're a tech-savvy solopreneur who wants full control over every automation and doesn't mind investing 30-40 hours in setup.

## HoneyBook

**Best for:** Creative solopreneurs (photographers, designers, coaches) who want simple, beautiful client workflows.

### Pricing

| Plan | Monthly Cost | What You Get |
|------|--------------|--------------|
| Starter | $36 | Basic features, limited automation |
| Essentials | $59 | More automation, more clients |
| Premium | $99 | Full feature access |

**Hidden costs:** None really. Pricing is straightforward. But meaningful automation requires Essentials or Premium.

### Setup time

**5-10 hours** for a basic setup. HoneyBook is designed for simplicity. You pick templates, customize them, and go.

### Strengths

- Beautiful, intuitive interface
- Quick setup
- Great for contracts and proposals
- All-in-one for creative workflows

### Weaknesses

- Limited advanced automation
- Pricing increased 89% in recent years
- Not built for complex service businesses
- Limited integrations

### Best use case

You're a photographer, designer, or coach with straightforward client workflows. You value simplicity over power. You don't need complex automations or custom integrations.

## Dubsado

**Best for:** Creative professionals who want more customization than HoneyBook without GHL's complexity.

### Pricing

| Plan | Monthly Cost | What You Get |
|------|--------------|--------------|
| Standard | $50 | Full feature access, 3 active clients |
| Premium | $80 | Unlimited clients, advanced features |

**Hidden costs:** None. Pricing is flat. But the 3-client limit on Standard means you'll upgrade quickly.

### Setup time

**10-20 hours** for a functional setup. Dubsado sits between HoneyBook and GoHighLevel. More power than HoneyBook. Less complexity than GHL.

### Strengths

- Highly customizable workflows
- Good template library
- Strong proposal and contract features
- Flat pricing (no per-message charges)

### Weaknesses

- Interface feels dated compared to HoneyBook
- Still requires DIY setup
- Automation builder has a learning curve
- "Dubsado setup specialist" is a job title on Fiverr for a reason

### Best use case

You're a creative professional who wants customizable workflows and doesn't mind spending 10-20 hours on setup. You've outgrown HoneyBook but don't need GHL's agency features.
