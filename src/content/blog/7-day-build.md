## What happens each day

### Day 1: Discovery Call + Business Mapping

We start with a 60-90 minute call. Not a sales pitch. A working session.

**We cover:**
- Your current tool stack (what you use, what you hate)
- Your client journey (lead → onboard → deliver → follow-up → invoice)
- Your pain points (where things fall through the cracks)
- Your non-negotiables (what must stay the same)

**What you need:**
- 60-90 minutes of focused time
- Access to your current tools (screen share)
- Honesty about what's broken

At the end of Day 1, we have a complete map of how your business runs. Not how you wish it ran. How it actually runs.

### Day 2: Workflow Design

We take the discovery map and design your workflows.

**We design:**
- Lead response workflow (how leads get contacted, when, by what channel)
- Onboarding workflow (what happens after a client says yes)
- Session workflow (reminders, prep, follow-up)
- Invoice workflow (when invoices go out, what happens if unpaid)
- Follow-up workflow (how cold leads get re-engaged)

You review the designs before we build. This is your chance to say "no, that's not how I do it" or "yes, exactly like that."

### Day 3: Configuration Begins

We start building. This is where most DIY setups fail — you're staring at a blank screen, trying to figure out which field maps to which.

We've done this before. We know what works.

**What we configure:**
- Your calendar sync (Google, Outlook, Apple)
- Your payment processor (Stripe, PayPal)
- Your communication channels (WhatsApp, Telegram, email)
- Your templates (invoices, reminders, follow-ups)
- Your triggers (what causes what to happen)

You don't touch anything yet. We build in our environment.

### Day 4: Build Continues + First Review

We finish configuration. Then we send you a walkthrough video.

**The video shows:**
- How leads flow through your system
- How clients get onboarded
- How invoices get sent and tracked
- How you'll get your morning briefing

You watch the video. You reply with questions or changes. Most clients say "looks good, one tweak" — and that's fine. This is your system. It should work the way you work.

### Day 5: Revisions + Testing

We make your changes. Then we test end-to-end.

**We test:**
- Lead form → instant response → calendar booking → confirmation
- New client → onboarding sequence → welcome packet → first session prep
- Session complete → follow-up → invoice → payment reminder (if needed)
- Unpaid invoice → day 3 reminder → day 7 reminder → day 14 escalation

If anything breaks, we fix it. If anything feels clunky, we smooth it out.

### Day 6: Final Review + Training Prep

We send you the final system walkthrough. You approve.

We also prepare your training materials:
- Quick reference guide (one-pager)
- Video library (2-5 minute clips for each task)
- Troubleshooting guide (what to do when X happens)

### Day 7: Handoff + You're Live

We hop on a 60-minute call. We walk through the system together. You ask questions. We show you how to approve things, how to override, how to check your morning briefing.

**At the end of the call:**
- Your system is live
- Your leads are being captured
- Your onboarding is running
- Your invoices are automated
- Your morning briefings are scheduled

You're done. No more setup. No more configuration. You're running. Your [daily morning briefing](/blog/morning-briefing) becomes your command center — one message telling you what needs attention.

## FAQ

**Q: What if my business is unusual?**
A: We've built systems for executive coaches, business consultants, health coaches, financial advisors, and marketing consultants. Every business is unusual in some way. The 7-Day Build is custom — we configure to how you work, not how a template says you should work.

**Q: What if I need changes after day 7?**
A: The first 30 days include unlimited tweaks. You discover edge cases as you use the system. We adjust. After 30 days, changes are billed separately — but most clients find the system works as-is.

**Q: Can I see the system before day 7?**
A: Yes. You get a walkthrough video on day 4 and day 6. You can request changes at each stage. The system doesn't go live until you approve it.

**Q: What if I'm not technical?**
A: Perfect. You're exactly who this is for. If you can send a text message and reply to emails, you can use what we build. The whole point is that you don't need to configure anything.

**Q: Do you work with my existing tools?**
A: If they're essential, yes. We integrate with your calendar, payment processor, and communication channels. If a tool is broken, we'll tell you — and we'll show you what to replace it with.
